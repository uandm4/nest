story
Designed by Bonderup & Thorup in 1968, the Gubi Semi Pendant Light is a best-selling Danish design that has gained worldwide recognition and iconic status.
Designed by Bonderup & Thorup, the Gubi Semi Pendant Light is a unique piece crafted from two enamelled metal quarter-circles, fused together, back-to-back, resulting in a distinctive cone-shaped composition. Diffusing an ambient downwards glow, with its balanced form and unique timeless design, it is extremely versatile, ideally situated above a dining table, in a living room or a bedroom. Available in a range of colourways and sizes, it adapts flawlessly to a range of contemporary interiors and can be displayed as a dynamic striking cluster or as a standalone piece. Also available is the Gubi Semi Pendant Light Metallic.

delivery
Great design is international. We deliver around the world to over 100 countries. Your order will travel by road, air or sea, depending on the size and fragility of your order.
If you have ordered before 2pm and the items you’ve ordered are in-stock and eligible to travel by courier your order will be dispatched the same day.
For any orders that contain fragile or oversized items our team will be in touch 14 days after placing your order, with an estimated time of arrival (ETA) to Nest. Once your items are all in-stock and have left the Nest warehouse, we will contact you with an ETA for delivery and arrival to you.
As we have a large range of products which vary in size, shape and weight, our international delivery charges can be subject to change. Due to the global situation with increasing fuel costs and other ongoing issues, we are also seeing price fluctuations which can affect our delivery charges. In these cases, we will contact you with the confirmed delivery charge and discuss the shipping options with you.
