story
Designed by Sir Kenneth Grange in his 90th year, the Anglepoise Type 80 Pendant Light is distinguished by its striking, graphic profile.
With each detail being meticulously considered and precision-engineered to deliver streamlined style, ease of use, and consistency across the Type 80 collection, this impressive light source exudes quality and gratification. Available in a beautiful range of colourways, the Type 80’s design is further enhanced by its attractive ‘halo’ light-escape feature on the diffuser, providing a soft glow. Ideally utilised as a cluster above a dining table or kitchen counter or as a stand-alone piece in a hallway or living environment, the Anglepoise Type 80 Pendant Light's versatile profile supplements a touch of colour and character to your home.

Kenneth Grange
Originally a draughtsman in an architect’s office and briefly an exhibition designer, Sir Kenneth Grange's career took off when Kodak asked him to design their Instamatic camera. Many of his designs have become familiar items, such as his food mixers for Kenwood, razors for Wilkinson Sword, pens for Parker and British Rail’s InterCity 125.
Sir Kenneth Grange became design director at the iconic British designer lighting brand Anglepoise in 2003. His first Anglepoise lamp was the Type 3™ desk lamp, which was launched in 2003. Grange has since gone on to design the Anglepoise Type 75 and Type 1228 collections.
