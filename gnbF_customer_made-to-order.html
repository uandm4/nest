What does ‘made to order’ mean?

When an item is labelled as ‘made to order’ it means that we do not currently have the item in stock at our UK warehouse.

The amount of time specified under ‘made to order’ is the estimated production lead time for the product. Estimated in weeks, this time period describes how long it will take the brand to manufacture your item.

These lead times are estimates, for your guidance and can be subject to change. A more accurate delivery estimate will be supplied to you by email within 14 days of your order placement.

Delivery to Nest

In addition to the ‘made to order’ time specified on a product page, please allow for up to two weeks for the items to be delivered from the brand to Nest.

Once delivered to us, your goods will be dispatched to you as quickly as possible. The shipping method and transit time applicable to your order will depend upon your location in the world, the delivery option chosen by you at checkout and the size and weight of the products purchased.

If you have any questions regarding delivery options, please see the FAQs on our delivery page or contact our team, who would be happy to help.

Your ‘made to order’ journey

Great design is most definitely worth the wait.

  1. 1. Browse our collection

    Browse and be inspired by our collection of furniture, lighting, home accessories and gifts.

  2. 2. Place your order

    With just a few clicks, place an order for your chosen piece of forever design. It’s as simple as that. If not in stock, the ‘made to order’ time listed on the product page will provide an estimate of how long your items will take to be manufactured by the brand.

  3. 3. After something a bit more bespoke?

    Many of our brands offer the ability to specify finish, size, and colour to suit you. This takes a little longer but it’s worth the way for your perfect piece. Contact our Sales Team for more information on bespoke finishes, not listed on our product page.

  4. 4. Made with love

    If your product is being made to order for you, our experienced brands craftspeople will begin manufacturing your product. When complete, your product will be shipped to Nest with updates of progress. Please allow for up to two weeks on top of the ‘made to order’ time for your items to be delivered to Nest.

  5. 5. We deliver worldwide

    Once your items arrive with us, we will dispatch at the earliest opportunity and you will be notified by Nest, or one of our delivery partners. If you would like more information surrounding delivery options, please see the FAQs on our delivery page or contact our team, who would be happy to help.

  6. 6. Our experts are on hand

    Our dedicated, professional specialists are on hand, Monday to Friday, 9am until 5pm (UK time) to help with any product advice, service questions or queries.
