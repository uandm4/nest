story
Designed by Danish design icons Hvidt & Molgaard, the &Tradition HM3 Drawn Dining Chair boasts a timeless, organic design.
Creating an atmosphere of wholesome honesty, the chair's inviting, elegant design highlights the enduring beauty of working with natural materials. Relying upon traditional craftsmanship techniques, the stunning dining chair is available in either White Oiled Oak, Soaped Oak or Oiled Walnut, with a beautiful hand-woven natural paper cord seat. A refined, sophisticated addition to a dining room, the HM3 Drawn Dining Chair's smooth curved backrest and slender, lightweight structure make it both a highly comfortable and practical addition to a contemporary interior space. The &Tradition Drawn Dining Chair is also available with armrests.

Hvidt & Molgaard
Peter Hvidt (1916-1986) and Orla Molgaard-Nielsen (1907-1993) were pioneers of Danish mid-century design and the founders of Copenhagen-based firm Hvidt & Molgaard.
Renowned for the simplicity of their works, the duo established a simple and precise aesthetic designing countless pieces of furniture over the years, many of which became icons of the era. Today, their work can be found exhibited at MoMA, Melbourne’s National Gallery, and Copenhagen’s Design Museum.
