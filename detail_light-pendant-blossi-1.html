story
Defined by a beautifully rounded glass shade, the Blossi Pendant Light, designed for Nuura by Sofie Refer, has been inspired by the golden light of the Nordic autumn.
With a soft, delicate appearance, the sophisticated pendant is both classic and contemporary in its style, creating an elegant hanging feature that can fit into an array of differently styled private and public spaces. Designed to give an indirect and soft light, the Blossi collection is defined by intricate metal details and the beauty of slender, curved glass, creating a minimalist yet eye-catching design. The light can be used perfectly as a single pendant or when clustered in lines or groups over a table or desk.

Sofie Refer
Sofie Refer was born in Denmark, 1974. Between 1998 - 2003 she studied at The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts Schools of Architecture, Design and Conservation - Institute of Product Design, Glass and Ceramics.
Her designs are based on Scandinavian simplicity combined with an extravagant look. Throughout her professional life she has focused on working with lighting design as she is fascinated by light in all aspects, convinced that lighting is the most important and powerful aspect in all environments.
She creates lighting design with the deepest respect and passion for light. In that process she constantly seeks for new ways to play with and express light, also by using new technology components and light sources.
