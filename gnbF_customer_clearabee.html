Furniture collection & recycling

We’ve partnered with Clearabee to help recycle your unwanted furniture. With all of us looking for ways to make more sustainable choices, we’re delighted to have partnered with Clearabee to offer an affordable, convenient and environmentally friendly way to recycle your unwanted furniture.

Clearabee

As the UK’s largest on-demand clearance company, Clearabee take their environmental obligations very seriously

In addition to the ‘made to order’ time specified on a product page, please allow for up to two weeks for the items to be delivered from the brand to Nest.

Once delivered to us, your goods will be dispatched to you as quickly as possible. The shipping method and transit time applicable to your order will depend upon your location in the world, the delivery option chosen by you at checkout and the size and weight of the products purchased.

If you have any questions regarding delivery options, please see the FAQs on our delivery page or contact our team, who would be happy to help.

How it works

To book this service you must be a Nest customer with a recent order or delivery. Simply attach a copy of your order or delivery note to your booking with Clearabee.

On the day of collection, Clearabee will send one of their friendly collection team to collect your old items of furniture, which can be inside or outside of your property. When our team are on the way, you’ll receive a text and email notification to keep you updated. Once removed, the removal team will take your item to a licensed facility for recycling and processing, you will receive a notification of this by email.
