story
Timeless and petit, the Lambert & Fils Dot 02 Pendant Light, designed by Samuel Lambert, embodies elegant industrial design.
Inspired by the beauty of simplistic geometric forms, the Lambert & Fils Dot 02 Pendant Light diffuses a refined light. Available in two striking finishes, its beautifully distinctive aesthetic is ideal for hanging low over a coffee or dining table, or for grouping in a cluster to form a dynamic lighting arrangement. Supplementing a touch of character and art decor to your home, this sculptural piece can efficiently illuminate your chosen space in style.
Dimensions
Height: 12.7cm x Diameter: 15.2cm
Materials & finishes
Brass or powder-coated steel and aluminium structure with glass diffusers. Nylon cord. Available in a range of colourways.
Light Source (Included)
1 x G9 3.5W LED 350LM 2700K CRI 80 IP20 rated.

If you have ordered before 2pm and the items you’ve ordered are in-stock and eligible to travel by courier your order will be dispatched the same day.

Samuel Lambert
Samuel Lambert ignited his design career refurbishing old lamps, giving a second life to vintage finds and learning about technicalities at the same time, he grew his knowledge and craft enough to start designing his own pieces.
Founding Lambert & Fils in 2010, named as a tribute to his late father and the craftsmanship that runs in the family, he now operates a collaborative lighting design studio in Montreal, designing and producing contemporary lighting with his peers.
