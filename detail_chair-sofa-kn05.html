story
The Knoll Studio KN05 Two Seater Sofa With Charcoal Legs, from Italian designer Piero Lissoni, is a piece of modern minimalist cool.
An elegant and simple sofa, the Knoll Studio KN05 Sofa With Charcoal Legs carries a genuine sense of luxury whilst maintaining a light tone. This particular configuration of the KN05 features bold charcoal steel legs, and luxurious upholstery in Kvadrat's Tonus fabric in a distinctive dark grey colourway. A beautiful example of quality contemporary seating furniture, the KN05 sofa will make a home of all manner of interior settings. The KN05 is part of Lissoni's ongoing collaboration with Knoll, and is produced in his native Italy.
Dimensions
Height: 70cm x Width: 150cm x Depth: 78cm
Seat Height: 38cm
Materials & finishes
Seat in polyurethane on a metal base in a charcoal finish, upholstered in Kvadrat Tonus fabric in Dark Grey 615 (90% new wool, 10% Helanca). Cushions are of variable density foam elements on the shell, with legs of charcoal-coloured steel.

Exact colouring and texture of the fabric can vary slightly. Fabric swatches are available on request and are recommended due to possible colour variations in imagery.

Piero Lissoni
Italian designer and architect Piero Lissoni received a degree in architecture at the Polytechnic of Milan. After completing his studies he worked as a designer for Molteni and Lema.
In 1986 he founded ‘Studio Lissoni’ with Nicoletta Canesi, developing projects in architecture, interior architecture, design and graphic design. The studio also works in the field of art direction and public relations. He has worked with a wide range of design houses and manufacturers including Driade, Kartell, Artemide and Lema.
