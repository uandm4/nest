story
The NORR11 Big Big Lounge Chair from Kristian Sofus Hansen & Tommy Hyldahl is a highly sumptuous and very modern object.
The larger-scale sibling to the iconic Little Big Chair, the NORR11 Big Big Chair features larger proportions that come with obvious benefits. Upholstered in beautiful Barnum boucle fabric in a range of colourways, and finished to perfection, the Big Big is inspired by classic Art Deco lounge chairs, and features a silhouette that is capable of looking aesthetically pleasing from all angles. Completed with a swivel base with an auto-return function, the Big Big Lounge Chair is certain to bring a sense of style and character to any living space.
Dimensions
Height: 98cm x Width: 96cm x Depth: 81cm
Seat height: 41cm
Materials & finishes
Frame of plywood & core of foam, with swivel base with auto return made of iron. Upholstered in Barnum fabric (45% Cotton, 15% Wool, 13% Acrylic, 19% Viscose, 8% Nylon) in a range of colour ways.
