story
Designed by Marc Sadler, the Twiggy Floor Lamp is a design classic from Italian lighting brand Foscarini.
Contemporary and striking, the Foscarini Twiggy Floor Lamp's remarkably flexible materials enable the formulation of a light source with impressive overall dimensions, whilst maintaining an extremely streamlined silhouette that harmonises its visual impact. Providing overhead, direct lighting, it is a practical lighting solution ideally utilised within a living space above a sofa, in a bedroom above an armchair or within a dining environment. Available in a range of colourways, it is part of the Foscarini Twiggy Collection of floor, ceiling and table lights. Available in a standard version or with innovative Foscarini MyLight technology.
Dimensions
Length: 170cm x Height: 211.5cm / 274cm Shade Diameter: 46cm x Shade Height: 29cm
Materials & finishes
Coated fiberglass-based composite material, PMMA, polycarbonate, coated metal and aluminum in a range of colourways. Transparent cable.
Lightsource (Included)
1 x 28W LED 2700K 3840LM CRI90 Dimmable (MyLight Version only) IP20 rated.

This product is CE approved only and should only be used in countries that follow and accept this standard. If it is used elsewhere it will be at the customer's sole risk, responsibility and liability.
MyLight Technology
Foscarini MyLight technology enables you to control your light via your phone or tablet using the Casambi app, available to download for free via the App Store or Google Play Store. Casambi detects your MyLight compatible lamps via Bluetooth and allows you to turn them on or off and regulate their luminous effects to suit your mood and desired ambience.

Marc Sadler
A designer, he's worked a long time in the sports sector, where he's experimented with new materials and innovative production processes. Has also worked successfully in furnishing and consumer products.
He is also active in the area of teaching, and holds conferences and workshops at design schools and universities in Italy and abroad (Domus Academy, Milan Polytechnic, etc.) He has received a series of awards including: DESIGN PLUS of Frankfurt for his Apotheos shower enclosure designed for Domino-Albatros (1995).
