story
The Tizio LED Table Lamp by Artemide is a strikingly modern design featuring adjustable, counterbalanced arms and head providing a unique silhouette which completes this contemporary task light. Winner of the Compasso d'Oro 1979.
Designed by Richard Sapper, the Tizio LED is a stunning addition to Artemide's lighting range. Its counterbalanced, adjustable arm and head allows you to position the lamp however you choose, enabling light to be cast over areas which require it. Whether used for task lighting, or as a bedside lamp, the Tizio is the perfect addition for those seeking a contemporary aesthetic without comprimising on build quality and technological ingenuity.

delivery
Great design is international. We deliver around the world to over 100 countries. Your order will travel by road, air or sea, depending on the size and fragility of your order.
If you have ordered before 2pm and the items you’ve ordered are in-stock and eligible to travel by courier your order will be dispatched the same day.
For any orders that contain fragile or oversized items our team will be in touch 14 days after placing your order, with an estimated time of arrival (ETA) to Nest. Once your items are all in-stock and have left the Nest warehouse, we will contact you with an ETA for delivery and arrival to you.
As we have a large range of products which vary in size, shape and weight, our international delivery charges can be subject to change. Due to the global situation with increasing fuel costs and other ongoing issues, we are also seeing price fluctuations which can affect our delivery charges. In these cases, we will contact you with the confirmed delivery charge and discuss the shipping options with you.

Richard Sapper
Richard Sapper was born in Munich in 1932 where he studied philosophy, graphics, engineering and economics. From 1956 to 1958 he worked in the Mercedes Benz project department. In 1958 went to Milan and worked for Gio Ponti and later for Rinascente.
Together with Marco Zanuso he designed radio and TV sets for Brionvega, the Grillo phone set for Siemens and various pieces of furniture, lamps etc.
